About Us

We aim to bring students joy in learning through an interactive feature in our website. Just as students wake up early in the morning, they have to log in to their zoom accounts everyday and go through long, mentally straining hours of lessons. We are here to change that! As students come into the website, they are immediately exposed to eye-catching visuals, which prepare their mind for upcoming lessons.

How it works

Once a student enters the website, they will enter the page called Landing Page (the page you're in now). Then, they can press the button 'Enroll' in order to add in their login details on another page, after which they will enter the interactive learning site. The interactive site will provide a game-like classroom environment, such that it keeps the students engaged and more keen to learn. Summary

This hack would promote a innovative and fun way of learning and attending lessons during this pandemic, turning a mundane and dry activity such as attending online lessons into an activity students would look forward to take part in. The idea of gamifying learning would definitely excite students and encourage them to stay motivated even when in an environment full of distractions.
